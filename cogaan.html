About COGAAN

COGAAN stands for CODING, GAMING, and ANIMATION. The Club is aimed at helping students get exposure to coding skills, the Software Industrial environment, and spreading awareness about the latest technological advancements.

Objectives

  • To Enlighten students about current software technology.
  • Helping students upskill in coding and soft skills.
  • Creating a platform for students to display their skills.
  • Conducting webinars & seminars to teach relevant software skills like web development, 3D modeling, and competitive coding.
  • Providing students the opportunity to create and develop products that can be used in the real-life world.
  • Build students' leadership skills by allowing them to lead the Club/Project teams.
  • To build a community where people can interact/Network with others.

Events and Activities

COGAAN conducted a total of 11 events, which included a total of 1,300 students with an average of 118 students per event.

  • COGAAN Club conducted a Web Development series (2 parts) where we taught students HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and helped them build a prototype website.
  • COGAAN conducted two collaborative events based on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (Hour of Code & Generative AI).
  • Project Forge was an initiative to encourage students to build sophisticated software projects/products in a collaborative/team environment.
