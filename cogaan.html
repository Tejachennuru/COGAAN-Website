About COGAAN

At COGAAN, we believe that coding, gaming, and animation are not just hobbies, but a way of life. We strive to create an environment where enthusiasts can come together and share their passion for these fields. Our club is run by students and for students, so we understand the unique needs and challenges of college life.

What We Offer

At COGAAN, we aim to provide a platform to showcase their skills and learn from their peers. We offer a variety of events and activities, including coding competitions, gaming tournaments, and animation workshops. Our experienced mentors and instructors are always available to help you improve your skills and achieve your goals.
